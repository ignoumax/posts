IGNOU (Indira Gandhi National Open University) is a A++ graded university by NAAC that provides distance and open learning in various undergraduate postgraduate programs

IGNOU is one of the largest University in the world and top 1 in India with enrollments upto 3+ million students in 2025

Students are provided their preferred study centres that are linked with the regional centres that faciliate they are studies

- Official website of IGNOU -  [ignou.ac.in](https://ignou.ac.in)
- [List of Regional centres](/posts/ignou-regional-centres)

## Admission

Students can enroll themselves for courses on by registering themselves and uploading the required documents on IGNOU portal, after successfull verification from the IGNOU side, they will confirm your admission and provide you a unique enrollment number

while most of the courses do not require a entrance exam, there are some courses that require you to clear the examination first for the admission in it 

|Course|Is Entrance exam required?|
|-|-|
|Bachelors, Masters, Certificate & Diploma|No|
|B.Ed, BSc Nursing & PhD courses|Yes|

## Courses

Students can enroll in the undergraduate (Bachelors), postgraduate (Masters), diploma, certificate and PHD courses. you can see the list of courses provided by IGNOU with their admission eligibility criteria [here](/posts/ignou-courses-lists)
