IGNOU (Indira Gandhi National Open University) is one of the largest University in the world and top 1 in India with enrollments upto 3+ million students in 2025

It provides distance and open learning in various undergraduate, postgraduate, certificate and diploma programmes

Students are provided their preferred study centres that are linked with the regional centres that faciliate they are studies

- Official website of IGNOU -  [ignou.ac.in](https://ignou.ac.in)
- [List of Regional centres](/posts/ignou-regional-centres)

## Admission

Students can enroll themselves for courses on by registering themselves and uploading the required documents on IGNOU portal, after successfull verification from the IGNOU side, they will confirm your admission and provide you a unique enrollment number

while most of the courses do not require a entrance exam, there are some courses that require you to clear the examination first for the admission in it 

|Course|Is Entrance exam required?|
|-|-|
|Bachelors, Masters, Certificate & Diploma|No|
|B.Ed, BSc Nursing & PhD courses|Yes|

## Courses

Students can enroll in the undergraduate (Bachelors), postgraduate (Masters), diploma, certificate and PHD courses. you can see the list of courses provided by IGNOU with their admission eligibility criteria [here](/posts/ignou-courses)

## Course Duration

Duration of each course depends on its type, whether it is a undergraduate course or postgraduate course or diploma or PG diploma or certificate course

|Type of Course|Minimum Duration|Maximum Duration|
|-|-|-|
|Undergraduate courses like BSc, BA, BCom etc|3 Years|6 Years|
|Postgraduate courses like MSc, MA, MCom etc|2 Years|4 Years|
|Certificate course|6 Months|1 Year|
|Diploma or PG Diploma|1 Year|3 Years|

> Check out the duration of a specific course provided by IGNOU [here](/posts/ignou-courses)

## FAQs

<details>
<summary>Is IGNOU degree valid?</summary>
Yes, IGNOU and its courses are recognised by UGC (University grant commission)
</details>

<details>
<summary>How classes are conducted in IGNOU?</summary>
Theory courses are not mandatory to attend, students can prepare from home for the semester exams

Some programs like BSc, MSc also have practical courses in which offline presence at the centre is compulsory
</details>

<details>
<summary>I am preparing for UPSC, will this degree make me eligible?</summary>
Yes, IGNOU degrees are recognised nationally and internationally. Most of the students prefer online distance learning from IGNOU so they can utilize their time to prepare for their upcoming government examinations more efficiently
</details>
